ABOUT US

Reach is an educational technology website designed by the students of BMS College Of Engineering and is one of its kind. It encourages budding engineering students in finding internships and Workshops of their interest. It also extends its offer to Industry experts and professionals in hosting Workshops to students, creating a congenial atmosphere that knits learning and fun. The Reach network is user-centric it enables the user to host as well as join based on their respective domain. It is free to use.
